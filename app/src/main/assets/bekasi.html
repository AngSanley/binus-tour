BINUSIAN Card Flazz
The BINUSIAN Card is an identity card for all BINUSIANS (students, alumni, teachers, training participants, and BINUS employees). As well as serving as an identity card, the BINUSIAN Card can be used to access information related to student activities or other campus activities online. BINUS employees can also use the BINUSIAN Card to obtain services related to their jobs. The BINUSIAN Card can also be used as a discount card at various stores. BINUS is working with a number of merchants, both within Jakarta and outside the city.
