Classroom
The Joseph Wibowo Center is a modern, smoke-free campus with 24 regular classrooms and seven theater-style classrooms, each equipped with AC, computer, LCD projector, whiteboard, sound system, and free internet access.

Library
LKC JWC was opened on September 2001, and is located on the fourth floor of the JWC building. It supports the learning processes of students and lecturers of BINUS BUSINESS SCHOOL Undergraduate and Graduate programs, and trainers and trainees of BINUS Continuing Education programs.

LKC JWC provides printed and electronic textbooks, reference books, theses, case studies, journals, magazines, newspapers, clippings, multimedia, and database collections, all of which are arranged according to Dewey Decimal Classification (DDC). It is complemented by facilities such as the official website, including a fast search engine for the whole collection, patron lockers, a computer room incorporating an English language self-access center, and Wi-Fi facilities to help patrons access the LKC website and electronic collections.

Auditorium
The auditorium, known as Room 310, is located on the third 3rd floor of JWC Campus. With dimensions of 15.5 x 8.5 meters, it can accommodate 80 – 100 persons and is equipped with modern sound system, LCD, computer and internet connection. This theater-style auditorium is usually used for guest lecturers or other important events.

Computer Laboratory
JWC Campus has three standard computer laboratories and another one for design programs. All laboratories are located on the fourth floor and are equipped with sophisticated and modern equipment and Internet connection.

Each computer laboratory has 33 computers equipped with the latest specifications. The design laboratory has 31 specialized computers for design (Macintosh).

Students can access the computer laboratories easily. As long as the laboratories are not being used for academic activities, lab work or other learning activities, they can be accessed at any time by students using their ID and password (provided to students upon joining BINUS.

Art & Design Laboratory
The Art & Design Laboratory is located on the fourth floor of the The Joseph Wibowo Center, and is equipped with high-end laboratory facilities using various hardware and software. It is divided into two sections: a computer room and a photography studio.

The computer room is fully equipped with up-to-date graphics and multimedia software and can perform intensive tasks for digital graphics and multimedia projects.

The photography studio is also equipped with high-end camera and lighting devices including professional digital SLR. It is used by students to create projects in producing and developing their assignment.

Wireless Internet

Musholla
Comfortable and peaceful, the musholla, located in The Joseph Wibowo Center (JWC), can accommodate 60 persons, and is used to organize Friday prayers. The musholla is equipped with 2 AC units, a book rack stocked with Islamic books, a headscarf rack, sound system and pulpit. The sound system is used to preach during the Friday prayers. Male and female ablution areas are separated by a curtain.

ATM
BCA ATMs are available on JWC campus, at the front of the building next to the security post. These are open from 7 p.m. to 10 p.m.

Parking Space
Parking space is available on three basement floor of the Joseph Wibowo Center, accommodating up to 243 cars and 150 motorcycles. Capacity can be increased to 290 cars if parallel parking is adopted.

Public Safety
Safety arrangements in The Joseph Wibowo Center (JWC) are excellent, with 32 CCTV cameras spread out in vital rooms such as public areas (lift, lobby, in and out exit), server room, computer lab, theater room and parking areas, ensuring that the campus remains safe. The security system in JWC uses BAS (Building Automatic System). Vehicles are controlled by the security team using Protecom, providing controlled access to the campus, using ID cards. A Lost and Found board containing information about goods found on campus is provided. Lost and found items can be retrieved in the BM (Building Management) room upon presentation of a valid ID card. The JWC campus is planning to add 16 more CCTV cameras in corridors and parking areas.

TV Wall
TV Wall is an integrated communication media which connects all BINUS Buildings. In the JWC building it is located in the main lobby, admission room, and the third and fourth floor central corridors. Information such as running texts and video format are displayed on the 42” LCD TV.
