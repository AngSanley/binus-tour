August 08 , 2012

fX BINUS INTERNATIONAL organized a special event for the Grand Launching of BINUS INTERNATIONAL fX Campus, Wednesday 8/8. CEO BINA NUSANTARA Group Bernard Gunawan, Managing Director BINUS, students, lecturers, parents, corporate partners, media and staff of BINUS INTERNATIONAL were present in this event.

In addition to getting to know each other better, the invitees also learned more about BINUS INTERNATIONAL. There are several majors at BINUS INTERNATIONAL fX Campus, namely Accounting & Finance, Art & Design, Business Management, Communication & Media and Computing.

With an area of 2,172.69 m², BINUS INTERNATIONAL fX is an extension of JWC Campus. In the first stage, 14 classrooms and one additional class for studio design were built. In the next stage, there will be seven more classrooms built.

There are also other facilities such as a library, photo copy center, student lounge and coffee shop. In addition, the campus will have a lecturer’s room, office and a pantry for staff and lecturers. The capacity of Binus fX is 1000 students.

The project was finished at the end of June 2012 and it will start operating in September 2012, for the odd semester 2012/2013 (17 September 2012). To support mobility, we provide a shuttle bus from BINUS INTERNATIONAL fX – BINUS INTERNATIONAL JWC Senayan.

With this new building, hopefully BINUS INTERNATIONAL fX Campus will provide better facilities and accessibility to support the students’ mobility.
