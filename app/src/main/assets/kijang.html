Classroom

Kijang Campus has 19 classrooms, each equipped with LCD projector, whiteboard and air-conditioning, as well as computers and internet access.

Copy Center

Located in Block B on Kijang Campus, the copy center caters to students, lecturers, and employees. There are photocopy, spiral and hardcover binding services. The copy center is open from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.

Futsal Courts

There is an outdoor futsal court located on Kijang Campus. The court's surface is made from cement and painted, to avoid causing injury to players. Its dimensions are international standard: 25m x 15m. Futsal has a large following at BINUS, and the court is used by Football Student Club for training sessions, as well as by students and employees. BINUS Football Club uses the court to host competitions among education institutions in the Jakarta area.

Canteen

The canteen on Kijang Campus offers a variety of Indonesian food and beverages at a reasonable price. It is located behind the campus building.

Parking Space

Many students bring their own vehicles to campus nowadays. However, finding a safe place to park can often be a problem. At BINUS, there is no need to worry.

Kijang Campus has parking space for 150 cars in the area surrounding campus buildings, as well as a private 100-motorbike parking lot for lecturers and employees.

The parking fee for cars is Rp 2000 for the first five hours and an additional Rp 1000 for each hour thereafter.

A parking sticker is also available for lecturers and employees.

Wireless Internet

Free wireless internet is available throughout the campus. On Kijang Campus there are hot spots around the badminton court.

Public Safety

BINUS campuses provide round-the-clock security service with 5-8 patrolling guards at each campus in two different shifts: morning and night. The security guards are assigned to several places such as parking lots, lobbies, and office-areas as well as inside the campus buildings.
